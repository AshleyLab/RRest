Datasets

The following datasets are available for use with the toolbox of algorithms:

  • Synthetic Dataset: Simulated ECG and PPG signals at a range of heart rates and respiratory rates to assist with algorithm development.

We hope to make the following datasets available for use with the toolbox in the future:

  • Vortal Dataset: Simultaneous ECG, pulse oximetry (photoplethysmography, PPG) and "gold standard" respiratory signals acquired from healthy volunteers.
  • Listen Dataset: Simultaneous ECG, PPG and "silver standard" respiratory signals acquired from acutely-ill hospital patients recovering from cardiac surgery.

The following datasets are publicly available, and can be used with the toolbox after some processing:

  • MIMIC II Dataset: Simultaneous ECG, PPG and "silver standard" respiratory signals acquired from intensive care patients.
  • CapnoBase Dataset: Simultaneous ECG, PPG and "gold standard" respiratory signals acquired from children and adults during elective surgery and routine anaesthesia.
