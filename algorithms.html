Respiratory rate algorithms

Research into respiratory rate algorithms has been hindered by a lack of open-source algorithms. As a result, the aim of this project is to provide open-source algorithms to facilitate future research. As stated in the licence accompanying the source code, the algorithms are not intended to be fit for any purpose. Instead, they act as an platform from which researchers can develop algorithms.
