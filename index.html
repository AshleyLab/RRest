Resources to aid research into estimation of respiratory rate from physiological signals

The Respiratory Rate Estimation project.

The Respiratory Rate Estimation project aims to aid research into the estimation of respiratory rate (RR) from commonly measured physiological signals.

This introduction to the project consits of the following:

  1. Background: The rationale for estimating respiratory rate from physiological signals.
  2. Algorithms: A toolbox of algorithms to aid research into estimation of respiratory rate.
  3. Datasets: Datasets which can be used with the toolbox of algorithms to evaluate both new and existing RR algorithms.
  4. Related Publications: A selection of publications which used the code and/or datasets referred to in this project.
  5. Contributions: Details of how to contribute to this ever-growing resource.
